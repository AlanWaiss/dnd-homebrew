You have done the near-impossible: you amused, impressed, or thoroughly confused the Mad Mage of Undermountain enough that he hasn't killed you. Instead, he has sitched a chaotic tether of raw arcane energy directly into your muscles.

This blessing manifests as jagged, glowing green veins that travel up your dominant weapon arm, pulsing rhythmically whenever tension rises.

The Mad Mage's Force. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a weapon attack or an [rules]Unarmed Strike[/rules], you can deal an extra 1d6 Force damage.

The Spark of Momentum. This blessing has 3 charges and regains all charges daily at dawn. When you take the [action]Attack[/action] action on your turn, you can expend 1 charge to force Halaster’s chaotic weave into your attacks. Roll a d6 to determine how his "respect" alters your martial prowess:
