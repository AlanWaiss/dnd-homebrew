## Description

<p>You gain access to the following additional tools:</p>
<p><em><strong>Drill.</strong></em> As a Bonus Action, you can spend 2 Tool Points to activate your Drill for 1 minute. While active, it counts as a simple melee weapon with which you are proficient. It deals 2d8 Piercing damage on a hit and is considered magical. On a hit, the target makes a Constitution save. on a failure, the target’s AC is reduced by 2 for one hour. <p><em><strong>Flash.</strong></em> As an action, you spend 2 tool points to release a blinding light. Each creature in a 30-foot [rules]Cone[/rules] makes a Dexterity saving throw. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage only.</p>
