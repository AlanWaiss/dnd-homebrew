## Snippet

<strong>Chainsaw.</strong> You can use a bonus action and spend 3 tool points to activate your chainsaw for 1 minute. While active, it counts as a simple melee weapon with which you are proficient. It deals <strong>3d6</strong> slashing damage on a hit and is considered magical.
On a critical hit, the target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a successful save, you can roll an additional <strong>2d6</strong> when determining the extra damage for a critical hit with a melee attack. On a failed save, the target&rsquo;s hit points are reduced to 0. Creatures that are huge or larger or targets with legendary resistances automatically succeed on their saving throw. Oozes and targets without a corporeal body such as ghosts are immune to this effect.
<strong>Debilitator.</strong> As a bonus action, you can spend 3 tool points to target a creature within 30 ft. The target must make a CON saving throw (DC {{savedc:int}}). On a failed save, the target becomes vulnerable to a random damage type for 1 minute.

## Description

<p>Your Tools gain the following benefits:</p>
<p><em><strong>Enhanced Weaponry.</strong></em> You roll an additional die when determining the damage of your Autocrossbow and Bioblaster.</p>
<p><em><strong>Chainsaw.</strong></em> You can use a Bonus Action and spend 3 Tool Points to activate your Chainsaw for 1 minute. While active, it counts as a simple melee weapon with which you are proficient. It deals 3d6 slashing damage on a hit and is considered magical.</p>
<p><em>Dismember.</em> If you roll a critical hit, the target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a successful save, you can roll an additional 2d6 when determining the extra damage for a critical hit with a melee attack. On a failed save, the target&rsquo;s hit points are reduced to 0. Creatures with a CR higher than your Artificer level or that have legendary resistances automatically succeed on their saving throw. Oozes and targets without a corporeal body such as ghosts are immune to this effect.</p>
<p><em><strong>Debilitator.</strong></em> This device makes a target vulnerable to a random damage type. As a bonus action, you can spend 3 tool points to target a creature within 30 feet. The target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, roll 1d10 and consult the table below. The target becomes vulnerable to the specified damage type for 1 minute.</p>
