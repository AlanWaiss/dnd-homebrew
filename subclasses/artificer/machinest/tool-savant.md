## Snippet

<strong>Chainsaw.</strong> You can use a Bonus Action and spend 3 Tool Points to activate your Chainsaw for 1 minute. While active, it counts as a simple melee weapon with which you are proficient. It deals 3d6 Force damage on a hit.
On a Critical Hit, the target makes a DC {{savedc:int}} CON save. On a success, roll an additional <strong>2d6</strong> when determining the extra damage. On a failed save, the target&rsquo;s hit points are reduced to 0. Creatures with a CR higher than your Artificer level or that have legendary resistances automatically succeed on their saving throw. Oozes and targets without a corporeal body such as ghosts are immune to this effect.
<strong>Debilitator.</strong> As a Bonus Action, you can spend 3 tool points to target a creature within 30 ft. The target must make a DC {{savedc:int}} CON saving throw. On a failed save, the target becomes vulnerable to a random damage type for 1 minute.

## Description

<p>Your Tools gain the following benefits:</p>
<p><em><strong>Enhanced Weaponry.</strong></em> You roll an additional die when determining the damage of your Autocrossbow,  Bioblaster, and Flash.</p>
<p><em><strong>Chainsaw.</strong></em> You can use a Bonus Action and spend 3 Tool Points to activate your Chainsaw for 1 minute. While active, it counts as a simple melee weapon with which you are proficient. It deals 3d6 Force damage on a hit.</p>
<p><strong>Dismember.</strong> When you score a Critical Hit with it against a creature, the target makes a Constitution saving throw. On a successful save, roll an additional 2d6 when determining the extra damage. On a failed save, the target&rsquo;s hit points are reduced to 0. Creatures with a CR higher than your Artificer level or that have legendary resistances automatically succeed on their saving throw. Oozes and targets without a corporeal body such as ghosts are immune to this effect.</p>
<p><em><strong>Debilitator.</strong></em> This device makes a target Vulnerable to a random damage type. As a Bonus Action, you can spend 3 tool points to target a creature within 30 feet. The target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, roll 1d10 and consult the table below. The target becomes Vulnerable to the specified damage type for 1 minute.</p>
