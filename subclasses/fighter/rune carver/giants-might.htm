As a Bonus Action, you imbue yourself with the might of giants, granting the benefits below for 1 minute or until you end them (no action required). You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses of it when you finish a [rule]Long Rest[/rule].

Giant Sized. If you are smaller than Large, you become Large, along with anything you are wearing. If you lack the room to become Large, your size doesn’t change.

Giant’s Strength. You have advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.

Gigantic Force. Once per turn, when you hit with an attack using a weapon or an [rules]Unarmed Strike[/rules], you can deal an extra 1d6 damage to the target.
