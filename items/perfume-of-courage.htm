This small glass bottle typically contains 1d6 + 2 uses of a golden perfume that smells of crushed citrus peals, bright ginger, and a metallic tang reminiscent of a freshly drawn blade or ozone right before a lightning strike. A creature can take a [action]Utilize[/action] action to spray the perfume onto itself or another creature within 5 feet. The target creature and each creature in a 5 foot Emanation originating from it gain Advantage on saving throws made to avoid or end the [condition]Frightened[/condition] condition. This effect lasts for 1 hour. At the DM’s discretion, the duration may be shortened if the creature gets wet.
