This cloak is woven from the silk of Phase Spiders and looks vaguely insubstantial, as if it's not entirely there.

Web Strider. While wearing this cloak, areas covered by webs do not count as difficult terrain for you and you have Advantage on Saving Throws against being restrained by webs.

Ethereal Escape. When you are hit by an attack while wearing this cloak, you can take a Reaction to briefly flicker into the Ethereal plane. The attacker rerolls the attack and must use the lower roll. If the attack still hits, the damage is reduced by 1d10. If the attack can affect creatures on the ethereal plane, this effect does nothing and is wasted. Once this property is used, it cannot be used again until the next dusk.
