This triangular prism is carved from a single piece of refined starlight. When held up to a light source, it projects a single, concentrated dot of violet light.

Celestial Refraction. You can use a Bonus action to direct the light at the fur of a Spirit Cat. The spirit cat can then use its Reaction to cast the [spells]Polymorph[/spells] using its Spellcasting trait, transforming itself into a [monsters]Displacer Beast[/monsters]. The spell does not require concentration when cast this way, but it only lasts for 1 minute. Once the spirit cat has cast [spells]Polymorph[/spells] this way, it cannot do so again until 2 days have passed.

Target Designator. You can use a Bonus action to direct the light at a creature or point within 200 feet. If you cast a spell that has a range of at least 5 feet while holding the prism, you can target the designated creature or point instead. Once you have used the prism to cast a spell this way, you can’t do so again until the next dawn.
