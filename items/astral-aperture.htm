This triangular prism draws raw energy from the Astral Sea and compresses it through a microscopic aperture to project a needle-thin beam of violet light, creating a tiny dot of light on a surface up to 200 feet away. The aperture can be socketed onto a staff or used as a Spellcasting Focus.

Celestial Refraction. You can use a Bonus action to direct the light at the fur of a Spirit Cat. The spirit cat can then use its Reaction to cast the [spells]Polymorph[/spells] using its Spellcasting trait, transforming itself into a [monsters]Displacer Beast[/monsters]. The spell does not require concentration when cast this way, but it only lasts for 1 minute. Once the spirit cat has cast [spells]Polymorph[/spells] this way, it cannot do so again until 2 days have passed.

Feline Distraction. As a Bonus action, you can direct the light at any surface within 60 feet of a cat or cat-like creature (DM’s discretion). A feline creature that sees the dot move must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom Saving Throw or use its next turn moving toward and attempting to "catch" the dot.

Target Designator. You can use a Bonus action to direct the light at a creature or point within 200 feet. If you cast a spell that has a range of at least 5 feet while holding the aperture, you can target the designated creature or point instead. Once you have used the aperture to cast a spell this way, you can’t do so again until the next dawn.
