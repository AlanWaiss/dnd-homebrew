This silver circlet has a magical pearl at its center and surrounded by two gemstones. When found, the circlet has 2 gemstones. While wearing the circlet, you can take a special [action]Attack[/action] action on your turn to summon a number of floating, spectral swords equal to the number of gemstones in the circlet and make one melee spell attack with each of them against a target within 10 feet of you. You are proficient with these attacks and use Charisma as your spellcasting ability for them. On a hit, the target takes Force damage equal to 1d8 + your Charisma modifier. The swords appear at your side and remain there until you dismiss them as a bonus action or until you have the [condition]Unconscious[/condition] condition or remove the circlet. While the swords are summoned, you can take this special Attack action once on each of your turns. The spectral swords shed bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet.

You can replace one of these melee spell attacks with a weapon attack as part of this special Attack action. If you’re able to make multiple attacks when you take the Attack action, you can instead replace a number of these spell attacks up to the number of weapon attacks that you can normally make as part of the Attack action.

When a hostile creature’s movement provokes an [action]Opportunity Attack[/action] from you, you can use your reaction to make one melee spell attack with one of the summoned swords against the creature, rather than making an opportunity attack.

This silver circlet has a magical pearl at its center and surrounded by two gemstones.

Adding Gemstones. A gemstone worth at least 5,000 gp can be added to the circlet over the course of 1 minute as it’s magically drawn and fastened into place. The circlet can hold a maximum of four gemstones.

Spectral Blades. When you take the [action]Attack[/action] action, you can choose to summon a number of floating, spectral swords equal to the number of gemstones in the circlet and attack. When you do so, make a Melee Spell Attack with each sword against a target within 10 feet of you, using Charisma for the attack rolls. On a hit, a sword deals Force damage equal to 1d8 + your Charisma modifier.

Persistent Presence. The swords remain at your side until you dismiss them (no action required), you have the [condition]Unconscious[/condition] condition, or you remove the circlet. While the swords are present, they shed Bright Light in a 10-foot radius and Dim Light for an additional 10 feet.

Martial Weaving. When you take the [action]Attack[/action] action provided by this item, you can replace one of the spectral sword attacks with a weapon attack. If you have the Extra Attack feature, you can replace a number of spectral sword attacks equal to the number of weapon attacks you can normally make.

Vigilant Strike. When a creature provokes an [action]Opportunity Attack[/action] from you, you can use your Reaction to make one Melee Spell Attack with a summoned sword against that creature instead of a normal Opportunity Attack.
