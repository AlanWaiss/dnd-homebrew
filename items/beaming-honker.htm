This peculiar fungus is easily identified by its bulbous, fleshy cap that resembles a bright red, over-inflated ball. It smells faintly of old pepper and damp earth.

Ingesting a Beaming Honker will cause your nose to glow for 1d4 hours, as if targeted by the [spells]Light[/spells] spell.

Alternately, when you take the [action]Attack[/action] action, you can replace one of your attacks with throwing the mushroom at a point you can see within 20 feet where it explodes, releasing bioluminescent spores. Each creature that breathes in a 10 foot radius [rules]Sphere[/rules] centered on that point must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or sneeze, potentially revealing their position. If a target fails this save by 5 or more, their nose also starts glowing for 1 minute and they cannot benefit from the [condition]Invisible[/condition] condition.
