This small container typically contains 1d6 + 2 uses of a deep purple powder. A creature can spend 10 minutes to apply the makeup onto itself or another creature. The target creature gains [sense]Darkvision[/sense] with a range of 30 feet. If the creature already has Darkvision, its range increases by 30 feet. This effect lasts for 8 hours or until the creature uses an Action to wipe the makeup off. At the DM’s discretion, the duration may be shortened if the creature’s face gets wet.
