This small container typically contains 1d6 + 2 uses of a glittery blue powder. A creature can spend 10 minutes to apply the makeup onto itself or another creature. The target creature gains the effect of the [spells]Detect Magic[/spells] spell for 1 hour or until the creature uses an Action to wipe the makeup off. At the DM’s discretion, the duration may be shortened if the creature’s face gets wet.
