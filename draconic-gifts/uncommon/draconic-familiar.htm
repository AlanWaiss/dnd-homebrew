Draconic Gift, Uncommon

You gain the aid of a draconic familiar, which might be a splinter of the consciousness of a dragon that invested you with power.

You can cast the [spells]Find Familiar[/spells] spell as a ritual without using any material components. When you cast the spell in this way, your familiar always takes the form of a [monsters]Pseudodragon[/monsters].

Additionally, when you take the [action]Attack[/action] action, you can forgo one of your own attacks to allow your pseudodragon familiar to make one attack of its own with its Reaction.
