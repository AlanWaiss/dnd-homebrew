Hill Rune (Level 7 or Higher). This Rune’s magic bestows a resilience reminiscent of a hill giant. While wearing or carrying an object that bears this Rune, you have Resistance to Poison damage and Advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the [condition]Poisoned[/condition] condition.

You can invoke the Rune as a Bonus Action, gaining Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage for 1 minute.
