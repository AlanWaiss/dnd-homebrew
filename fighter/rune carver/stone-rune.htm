This Rune’s magic channels the judiciousness associated with stone giants. While wearing or carrying an object inscribed with this Rune, you have Advantage on Wisdom ([skill]Insight[/skill]) checks, and you have [sense]Darkvision[/sense] with a range of 120 feet.

When a creature you can see ends its turn within 30 feet of you, you can take a Reaction to invoke the Rune and force the creature to succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [condition]Charmed[/condition] condition for 1 minute. While Charmed in this way, the creature has a speed of 0 and has the [condition]Incapacitated[/condition] condition, descending into a dreamy stupor. The creature repeats the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.
