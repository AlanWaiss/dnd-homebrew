Using this Rune, you can glimpse the future like a storm giant seer. While wearing or carrying an object inscribed with this Rune, you have Advantage on Intelligence ([skill]Arcana[/skill]) checks, and you can’t be Surprised as long as you don’t have the [condition]Incapacitated[/condition] condition.

You can invoke the Rune as a Bonus Action to enter a prophetic state for 1 minute or until have the [condition]Incapacitated[/condition] condition. Until the state ends, when you or another creature you can see within 60 feet of you makes an attack roll, a saving throw, or an ability check, you can take a Reaction to cause the roll to have Advantage or Disadvantage.
