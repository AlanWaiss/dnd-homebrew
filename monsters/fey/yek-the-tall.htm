Villain Actions

Yek has three villain actions. He can take each action once during an encounter after an enemy’s turn. He can take these actions in any order but can use only one per round.

Action 1: What Are You Waiting For?! Each ally within 60 feet of Yek who can hear him can move up to their speed or make a melee weapon attack (no action required).

Action 2: Focus Fire. Yek chooses an enemy he can see with 60 feet of him. Yek and each ally within 60 feet of him who can hear him can move up to their speed toward the target.
