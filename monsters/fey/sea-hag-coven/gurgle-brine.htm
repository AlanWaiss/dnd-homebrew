Gurgle Brine, Sea Hag Oracle

Traits

Amphibious. Gurgle can breathe air and water.

Coven Magic. While within 30 feet of at least two hag allies, Gurgle can cast one of the following spells, requiring no Material components, using the spell’s normal casting time, and using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 15): [spells]Augury[/spells], [spells]Find Familiar[/spells], [spells]Identify[/spells], [spells]Locate Object[/spells], [spells]Scrying[/spells], or [spells]Unseen Servant[/spells]. Gurgle must finish a Long Rest before using this trait to cast that spell again.

Fish Voice. Gurgle is completely silent and cannot physically speak. Her speech, including Verbal components for her spells, is magically projected through the mouth of any dead fish or Grimlock within 60 feet of her.

Vile Appearance. Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 14, any Beast or Humanoid that starts its turn within 30 feet of Gurgle and can see her true form. Failure: The target has the [condition]Frightened[/condition] condition until the start of its next turn. Success: The target is immune to Gurgle’s Vile Appearance for 24 hours.
