Traits

Careful Spell (1/Day). When the sorcerer casts a spell that forces other cretures to make a saving throw, they can choose up to 3 creatures. The chosen creatures automatically succeed on their saving throws against the spell, and they take no damage if they would normally take half damage on a successful save.

Transmuted Spell (1/Day). When the sorcerer casts a spell that deals a type of damage from the following list, they can change that damage type to one of the other listed types: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison, Thunder.

Wild Magic. When the sorcer casts a spell of Level 1 or higher, roll on the Wild Magic Surge table.
