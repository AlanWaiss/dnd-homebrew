Traits

Scrappy Survivor. Sylvia is proficient with Simple weapons and Martial weapons that have the [wprop]Finesse[/wprop] or [wprop]Light[/wprop] properties and can wear Light armor.

Structural Scavenger. Allies within 10 feet of Sylvia have a +3 bonus to Wisdom ([skill]Perception[/skill]) or Intelligence ([skill]Investigation[/skill]) checks to find hidden compartments or secret doors.

Vermin’s Whisper. Sylvia can spend 10 minutes communicating with local rats. She can learn the general layout of nearby tunnels, the presence of "large predators," or if anyone has passed through recently.

Actions

Multiattack. Sylvia makes two attacks, using Scratch or Hand Crossbow in any combination. It can replace one attack with a Bite attack.

Bonus Actions

Shape-Shift. Sylvia [rules]shape-shifting;shape-shifts[/rules] into a Medium rat-humanoid hybrid or a Small rat, or it returns to its true humanoid form. Its game statistics, other than its size, are the same in each form. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn’t transformed.

Reactions

Rodent’s Reflex (3/Day). Trigger: Sylvia is hit by a melee attack roll while in humanoid or hybrid form. Response: Sylvia uses her Shape-Shift ability to transform to her rat form and move up to her speed without taking [action]Opportunity Attack;Opportunity Attacks[/action]. The attacker must reroll the attack and if Sylvia moves out of range, the new attack roll is made with Disadvantage.
