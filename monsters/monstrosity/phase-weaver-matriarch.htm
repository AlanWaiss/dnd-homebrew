Traits

Ethereal Sight. The matriarch can see 120 feet into the Ethereal Plane while on the Material Plane and vice versa.

Flicker Defense. Attack rolls against the matriarch have Disadvantage as she blurs between planes. This trait is suppressed while the matriarch has the [condition]Incapacitated[/condition] condition.

Spider Climb. The matriarch can climb difficult surfaces, including along ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

Web Walker. The matriarch ignores movement restrictions caused by webs, and the matriarch knows the location of any other creature in contact with the same web.

Bonus Actions

Reactions

Quick Phase. Trigger: The matriarch is hit by an attack roll. Response: The matriarch teleports to an unoccupied space she can see within 30 feet. The target space must be connected to the matriarch’ current space by webs.

Villain Actions

The matriarch has three villain actions. She can take each action once during an encounter after an enemy’s turn. She can take these actions in any order but can use only one per round.

Action 1: Ethereal Ambush. The Matriarch and up to five allied Phase Spiders within 60 feet of her use Ethereal Jaunt as a reaction. Each spider then moves up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks. One of her allies can then make an attack.
