Reactions

Villain Actions

Ghanash has three villain actions. He can take each action once during an encounter after an enemy’s turn. He can take these actions in any order but can use only one per round.

Action 1: Feast, My Children! Ghanash lets out a bone-chilling howl. He and any allied Ghouls or Zombies within 60 feet can move up to their speed toward the nearest creature that isn't an Undead without provoking opportunity attacks and make 1 attack. If any of these attacks have an effect that allows a saving throw, the saving throw is made with Advantage.

Action 2: The King’s Binding. Ghanash strikes his blade against the ground, releasing a pulse of necrotic energy. Each creature of Ghanash’s choice within 30 feet must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, a creature takes 14 [rollable](4d6);{"diceNotation":"4d6","rollType":"damage","rollDamageType":"Necrotic","rollAction":"The King's Binding"}[/rollable] Necrotic damage and has the [condition]Paralyzed[/condition] condition until the end of its next turn. On a success, it takes half damage and its speed is halved until the end of its next turn..

Action 3: Grave Hunger. Ghanash commands the literal earth to consume his foes. Skeletal hands claw up from the ground in a 20-foot radius centered on him. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw or have the [condition]Restrained[/condition] (save ends). Additionally, Ghanash immediately makes one Life Drain attack against every Restrained or Paralyzed creature within his reach. He regains hit points equal to the total Necrotic damage dealt by these specific attacks.
