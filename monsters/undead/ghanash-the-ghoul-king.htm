Bonus Actions

Reactions

Fanatical Defense. Trigger: Ghanash is hit by a melee attack roll while within 5 feet of a willing ally. Response: The ally interposes itself and becomes the target of the attack.

Villain Actions

Ghanash has three villain actions. He can take each action once during an encounter after an enemy’s turn. He can take these actions in any order but can use only one per round.

Action 1: Feast, My Children! Ghanash lets out a bone-chilling howl. Any allied Ghouls or Zombies within 60 feet can move up to their speed toward the nearest creature that isn't an Undead without provoking opportunity attacks and make 1 attack. If any of these attacks have an effect that allows a saving throw, the saving throw is made with Advantage.

Action 2: The King’s Binding. Ghanash strikes his blade against the ground, releasing a pulse of necrotic energy. Spectral hands then reach up from the ground. Each creature of Ghanash’s choice within 30 feet must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw.
