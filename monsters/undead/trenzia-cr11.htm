Villain Actions

Trenzia has three villain actions. She can take each action once during an encounter after an enemy’s turn. She can take these actions in any order but can use only one per round.

Action 1: Arise, My Masterpiece! Trenzia fires a concentrated beam at her Flesh Golem Juggernaut. The Golem immediately uses its Thunderous Leap (if available) or moves its full speed and makes one Slam attack.

Every creature wearing metal armor or holding a metal weapon within 30 feet must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be pulled 15 feet toward her. She then makes one Arcane Claw attack against each creature within 5 feet of her.
