You have been blessed by infernal powers granting you the following benefits.

Devilish Resitance. You have Resistance to Fire damage.

Devilish Charm. When a creature you can see within 60 feet of you targets you with an attack, you can take a Reaction to force that creature to make a Charisma saving throw with a DC of 12 plus your proficiency bonus. On a failed save, the creature has the [condition]Charmed[/condition] condition until the start of its next turn, and you choose a new target for the triggering attack. You must be able to see the new target and the new target must be within the range of the triggering attack. On a successful save, the triggering attack is made with Disadvantage. Once you use this trait, you can’t use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
