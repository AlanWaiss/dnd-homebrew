As a Bonus Action, you can choose one creature that you can see within 60 feet of you. That creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 8 + your Proficiency Bonus + your Transformation Stage) or be branded with a fiery mark that remains on them for 1 minute.

Choose one of the following effects:

  • The creature takes a -2 penalty on all Saving Throws.
  • The first attack against the creature on each turn is made with [rule]Advantage[/rule].
  • The creature cannot regain Hit Points.

You can use this ability a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all uses when you finish a Long Rest.
